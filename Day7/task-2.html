Chicken Biryani Recipe

How to Cook Chicken Biryani :

  1. Soak the Rice: Rinse 2 cups of basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Fry the Onions: Thinly slice and fry 2 onions until golden brown. Set aside.
  4. Cook the Chicken: In a pot, cook the marinated chicken until 70â€“80% done.
  5. Parboil the Rice: Boil soaked rice with whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain.
  6. Layer the Biryani: Layer cooked chicken with rice, fried onions, mint, and saffron milk.
  7. Steam (Dum) Cooking: Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  8. Serve: Let it rest for 10 minutes, then fluff and serve with raita.
